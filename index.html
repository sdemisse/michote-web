What is Michote ?

Very simple and easy to use mobile app. Custom designed to suit Ethiopian and Eritrean community in North America. If you have a spare space at your house or looking for a roommates you can post it with easy steps. Thousands of people in your community will see you ads

Why choose Michote?

  • Simple and accurate address search by city and state
  • Private message build around maintaining your privacy
  • You can save any number of ads in your favorite list to look back for later reference
  • Easy way to update or deactivate your ad at anytime
  • 24/7 available customer support and more
    • What are the steps to create new Ad?

    It's very easy to create a new ad, Go to profile then press post new ads. You will be prompted with step-by-step screens to add all the necessary information including the type of your property, your ideal renter preference and monthly rent, utilities you will include with the rent, rules you want to enforce, and pictures of your listing. if you want to add more information add to the description with any language you want.\n

    Which of my information will be public?

    We care about your personal information seriously. Your address and full name will not be available for the public. we only show the 4-mile radius of your listing, your first name, and initial from your last name, and your public profile image for potential renters.\n

    How long it will take to verify my Ad?

    Usually, it will take less than 24 hours for your ad to be verified and available. You can always check the status of your ad by going to My ads and click on the ad from the list. If you feel your ad takes more than the expected time please contact us.\n

    What kind of property I can advertise?

    You can post ad to rent a basement, to find a roommate for a private room in multiple bedroom apartment or house. You can have ad to share a room\n
